Experience

Taking a tattoo machine for the first time 14 years ago, after getting my first tattoo I began this journey which you never stop learning, in a self-taught way I specialized in realism and black and white tattoos, not leaving aside the other styles but without a doubt that would be my forte in this art, I started tattooing fruits and mousepad to perfect the technique until I took the big step of having the first client, like many other tattoo artists you start with friends, who are the ones who they give you the confidence to experiment on them.

After 14 years, after so many designs, ink and good people that I have been able to capture my art, I am still looking to perfect it beyond itself, studying photography, oil painting, graphic design. This knowledge is an enhancer for my work, adding value to the way you receive it and you can see the high quality in which it develops.
