About Me

Born in Venezuela in 1993, in a city near the capital, a young 18-year-old Jesus entered the world of tattooing after doing graffiti throughout his adolescence, turning his art from walls to skin.

Jesus specialized in realistic black and white tattoos and portraits, so that the client can have that close connection with him, taking those special tattoos to create an emotional moment during and after it.

Right now Jesus lives in Miami Fl, Usa. working with great artists and in different stores looking to continue growing and learning about this culture which is what keeps him awake every day.
